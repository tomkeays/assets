Start by search the local collection, but if we don't own what you are looking for, explore other ways to request it from other regional and national libraries.

  • Search the Le Moyne Catalog to find books, videos, audio, and other materials in our collection. See what's new in our monthly New Titles listing.

  • Search Connect NY a consortium of academic libraries in New York. Books are delivered within 3 business days. You need a current Le Moyne ID card to initiate a request for a book.

  • Use an Interlibrary Loan form to obtain books not found in Le Moyne's catalog or in Connect NY.

  • Search Worldcat to see what libraries beyond our region have a copy of the book. It is good way to get information about the item that you need in filling out your Interlibrary Loan request.

What does this search?

  • Click the "Le Moyne Catalog" option to search the library's catalog for books, eBooks, digital records, images, periodicals, and more.

  • Click the "Connect NY Catalog" option Search Connect NY to find a copy of a book that Le Moyne does not own or which is unavailable. Items requested through Connect NY are usually available within 3 working days.

  • Items not found at Le Moyne and not available through Connect NY can be ordered from another library by filling out an Interlibrary Loan Request Form.
